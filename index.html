Welcome to the site of Evergreen Glee Club, a men's glee club in the Puget Sound area, established in January 2006.

We sing a varied repertoire of nostalgic, traditional and contemporary Japanese songs. Members practice twice a month, ususlly on Sundays for two hours.

Please follow us on Facebook to get latest updates!
